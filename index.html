About

I'm Jeffery Beach, a Hawaii-based composer and guitarist exploring what becomes possible when you expand the boundaries of the instrument.

"The 7-string isn't about showing off an extra string—it's about accessing emotional and sonic dimensions that don't exist on a traditional guitar. It changes what you can say."

My work blends the contemplative tradition of classical guitar with sonic experimentation—alternative tunings, extended techniques, and layered textures that create music existing between acoustic and electronic, between ancient and future.

Influenced deeply by the ECM Records aesthetic, where space and silence are as composed as the notes. Where the goal is not complexity but genuine emotional resonance. Where a piece should transport you somewhere unexpected.

The music ranges from mystical and haunting to airy and transcendent. It's meant for deep listening—music that rewards patience and presence.
